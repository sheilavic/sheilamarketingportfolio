ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Sheila! I recently graduated from the University of Southern California (USC), with a bachelors degree in Business Administration and a minor in Consumer Behavior.

I’ve always been fascinated by how people think, whether it’s through stories in books and movies or the choices they make every day. That curiosity naturally led me to marketing, where I get to combine creativity and strategy to make ideas meaningful and impactful.

With experience across product management, financial analysis, branding, and consulting, I love turning insights into thoughtful strategies that connect with people and drive growth. I’m especially passionate about understanding what moves consumers and using data to tell stories that inspire action.

Outside of work, you can usually find me playing board games, doing Pilates, or baking something sweet to share with friends. Something you may not know about me is that I grew up living in four different countries! I have lived in Laos, Thailand, Ethiopia and Italy.
